Breaking Down Enterprise Silos with AI

Modern enterprises run Dynamics 365 across multiple modules — Sales, Finance, Supply Chain, Customer Service, Field Service, Commerce, Human Resources, and Customer Insights. Yet the most impactful business processes don’t live in a single module: they span the entire organization. Order-to-Cash flows from Sales through SCM to Finance. Employee onboarding touches HR, Payroll, IT, and Teams. Customer retention requires coordinating Service, Marketing, and Commerce simultaneously.

The 120 Cross-App AI Agents in this catalog represent a new paradigm: autonomous, intelligent orchestrators that understand cross-functional business intent and coordinate actions across D365 modules, the Power Platform (Power Automate, Power Apps, Power BI, Copilot Studio), Microsoft 365 (Teams, Outlook, Excel, SharePoint), and Azure AI services.

Each agent leverages Dataverse as the unified data backbone, Azure OpenAI for intelligence, and the Microsoft Copilot extensibility framework for seamless user interaction. The result: faster cycle times, fewer errors, and dramatically improved cross-functional visibility for decision-makers at every level.
